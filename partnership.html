Our Partners

Velantis Aviation have partnered with Geven to deliver flexible seat and interior leasing solutions. With a range of options ranging from economy class and premium economy class to business class seats, we have the funding capacity to deliver over 100 shipsets per annum.

How our partnership with Geven works:

- Airlines want Geven’s new range of seats but don’t want the capital expenditure

- Geven agrees seat order specification and quantity with airline as normal

- Velantis supplies highly competitive lease rate per seat per flight hour

- Lease rate agreed with airline

- Geven manufacture seats to customer requirement

- Velantis pay Geven immediately for seats in full

- Velantis pay Geven finance commission to enhance profitability

- Airline gets the seats they want without one off capital expenditure

- Geven concentrates on getting more airlines and manufacturing more seats
