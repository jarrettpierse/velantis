Aviation Seat Leasing Solutions

About

Velantis is a specialist aviation leasing company based in Dublin which has been established to focus on leasing seats. The senior team, who have many years of experience in aviation finance and aviation interiors, recognized the need to provide this leasing option for seats and have produced an innovative solution matching the cost of the seat on a block hour basis to the revenue generated by the aircraft seat.

This leasing solution is designed to take large capital expenditure out of the new seat procurement decision process, smoothing cashflows for airlines and making it easier to buy new seats, rebrand and refresh the cabin.

What We Do

Aviation Seat Leasing

The seat leasing concept is continuing to gather pace in the aircraft interiors sector. We have a range of solutions for airlines.

Financial Solutions

We have a range of financial solutions where airlines will be able to undertake seat leasing whether they are: Fitting out a new aircraft, Refreshing or reconfiguring an existing aircraft or completing a sale and leaseback of the seats on an existing aircraft.

Improved Cashflow

By taking large capital expenditure out of the new seat procurement decision process, we enable airlines to smooth their cashflows and match the cost of the seat to the revenue generated by the aircraft.
